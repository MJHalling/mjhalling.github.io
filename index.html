Through racing partnerships, Halling Thoroughbreds has campaigned more than thirty horses, including five stakes winners and two Grade 1 winners. Based in Del Mar, our horses have competed across the country and around the world, with starts at Royal Ascot, Newmarket, Chantilly, Meydan, and three Breeders’ Cup appearances.
